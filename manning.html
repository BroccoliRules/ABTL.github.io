About Manning's Equation

Manning's Equation is composed of the cross-sectional area of flow, channel slope, hydraulic radius, and Manning's Roughness Coefficient.

The cross-sectional area and hydraulic is calcuated thorugh the width and depth of the channel. The slope is a measured value. The roughness coefficient depends on what material the channel is made from.
